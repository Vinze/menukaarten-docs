    Introduction

    Welcome at our technical design and developer manual of this framework. For our school project we needed to build a menukaarten (menu cards) application. After doing some research on existing frameworks we decided to build our own, which is documented on these web pages. The section Framework contains information about the workings of this framework. The section Menukaarten.nl contains information specific to the meukaarten application. And at the section Decisions & Research you can find our justification of the design choices we have made.

    For more information you can contact Vincent Bremer (vbremer89[at]gmail[dot]com or Douwe de Haan (douwedehaan[at]live[dot]nl)
